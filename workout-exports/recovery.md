## RCV-001 — Mobility Flow

Category: recovery | Type: recovery | Variant: —
Difficulty: beginner | Duration: 30 min
Equipment: bodyweight
Focus: mobility, hips, T-spine, ankles
Tags: recovery, mobility, taper_phase, beginner

Description: Full-body mobility to restore range of motion after hard hybrid weeks.

Progression: No progression on load — add 5 min easy movement only if you finish feeling fresher than you started. If soreness is high (RPE 7+ walking), swap to mobility-only for the day.

## RCV-002 — Recovery Run

Category: recovery | Type: recovery | Variant: —
Difficulty: beginner | Duration: 35 min
Equipment: running
Focus: active recovery, easy pacing, legs
Tags: recovery, running, zone2, active_recovery, taper_phase, beginner

Description: Very easy jog to promote blood flow without adding training stress.

Progression: Active recovery only — if HR drifts above easy jog, walk 2 min. No progression except +5 min when sleep and soreness are green.

RPE: RPE 3–4 throughout — you should finish feeling better than when you started.

Coaching cues:
  - Stay tall — think "hips forward," not leaning from the waist.
  - On 1 km repeats, start 2–3 sec slower than race pace; negative split if you can.
  - First 200 m off the rower/ski: quick cadence, then settle — don't sprint the transition.
  - Arms relaxed below chest; drive the knee, don't overstride.

Substitutions: Match the written effort, rest, and rep scheme. Substitutions are for missing equipment — not to make the session easier.

No treadmill needed outdoors. Treadmill: set 1% incline for easy/tempo work. Bike or row: use 1 min hard ≈ 200 m run as a rough swap in mixed sessions.

Any easy modality (walk, bike, row, swim) at the same duration and RPE 3–4 is fine.

--- SESSION ---

Warm-up:
5 min walk

Main set:
20–25 min jog @ truly easy (Zone 1)

Block 2:
Walk 1 min every 8 min if legs feel heavy

Block 3:
No strides, no surges

Cool-down:
5 min walk + calf stretch

## RCV-004 — Row Flush

Category: recovery | Type: recovery | Variant: —
Difficulty: intermediate | Duration: 30 min
Equipment: rower
Focus: rowing recovery, posture, breathing
Tags: recovery, rowing, active_recovery, taper_phase, intermediate

Description: Easy rowing to open the upper back and hips without race intensity.

Progression: No progression on load — add 5 min easy movement only if you finish feeling fresher than you started. If soreness is high (RPE 7+ walking), swap to mobility-only for the day.

RPE: RPE 3–4 throughout — you should finish feeling better than when you started.

Coaching cues:
  - Sequence: legs → back → arms; recovery is arms → back → legs.
  - Grip the handle with relaxed fingers — death grip costs the farmer carry later.
  - Target 24–28 spm for race pieces; 18–22 spm for aerobic work.
  - Sit tall at the finish; don't collapse the chest between strokes.

Substitutions: Match the written effort, rest, and rep scheme. Substitutions are for missing equipment — not to make the session easier.

No rower: SkiErg same distance/time, or bike 90 sec moderate per 500 m row in metcons.

Any easy modality (walk, bike, row, swim) at the same duration and RPE 3–4 is fine.

--- SESSION ---

Warm-up:
3 min very easy row

Main set:
20 min row @ 18–20 spm, easy pressure

Block 2:
Focus: long strokes, relaxed grip

Block 3:
Substitute: 25 min easy walk if no rower

Cool-down:
5 min easy row

## RCV-005 — Ski Flush

Category: recovery | Type: recovery | Variant: —
Difficulty: intermediate | Duration: 30 min
Equipment: ski_erg
Focus: shoulder recovery, technique, easy aerobic
Tags: recovery, ski_erg, active_recovery, taper_phase, intermediate

Description: Light ski erg to keep shoulders moving without training load.

Progression: No progression on load — add 5 min easy movement only if you finish feeling fresher than you started. If soreness is high (RPE 7+ walking), swap to mobility-only for the day.

RPE: RPE 3–4 throughout — you should finish feeling better than when you started.

Coaching cues:
  - Long arms at the catch; snap the handle past the knees, then finish with triceps.
  - Breathe out on the pull — avoid holding breath through the drive.
  - Legs do 60% of the work; save arms for the last 250 m of each piece.
  - Damper 5–7 for most HYROX prep; higher is not always faster.

Substitutions: Match the written effort, rest, and rep scheme. Substitutions are for missing equipment — not to make the session easier.

No SkiErg: row the same distance/time at the same RPE. Bike: 2 min moderate ≈ 500 m ski in conditioning blocks.

Any easy modality (walk, bike, row, swim) at the same duration and RPE 3–4 is fine.

--- SESSION ---

Warm-up:
3 min easy ski

Main set:
20 min easy ski — smooth rhythm

Block 2:
Break into 4×5 min if shoulders fatigue

Block 3:
Substitute: easy row same duration

Cool-down:
5 min walk + shoulder stretch
