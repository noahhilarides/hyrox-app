## STR-003 — Full Body Strength

Category: full_body_strength | Type: strength | Variant: full_body
Difficulty: intermediate | Duration: 65 min
Equipment: barbell, pull-up bar, dumbbells
Focus: deadlift, pressing, pulling, carry capacity
Tags: strength, full_body, deadlift, pressing, carry, build_phase, intermediate

Description: Balanced full-body strength for hybrid athletes.

Progression: Add 2.5–5 kg or one rep per set when working sets are RPE 7 with 2 reps in reserve. Deload every 4th week if soreness stacks across squats and running. In build phase, progress one variable at a time (load, reps, or density).

Coaching cues:
  - Tall posture, shoulders packed — don't shrug to your ears.
  - Short, quick steps; look 10 m ahead, not at your feet.
  - Grip: crush the handle in the first 10 m, then breathe and relax forearms slightly.
  - Turn corners wide and slow — a dropped implement is slower than a controlled turn.

Substitutions: Match the written effort, rest, and rep scheme. Substitutions are for missing equipment — not to make the session easier.

No implements: suitcase carry (DB/KB) same distance each hand, or sandbag bear-hug carry.

--- SESSION ---

Warm-up:
5 min row or bike · Movement prep: deadlift pattern, scap pull-ups

Main set:
Deadlift — 5×5 @ RPE 8

Block 2:
Bench Press — 4×6

Block 3:
Pull-Ups — 4×AMRAP (leave 1–2 reps in reserve)

Block 4:
Farmer Carry — 4×40m

Cool-down:
Full-body stretch

## STR-004 — Goblet Foundation

Category: full_body_strength | Type: strength | Variant: full_body
Difficulty: beginner | Duration: 40 min
Equipment: dumbbells, kettlebell_optional
Focus: squat pattern, hinge pattern, core bracing
Tags: strength, legs, foundation, base_phase, beginner

Description: Beginner-friendly squat and hinge — learn patterns you will use under fatigue at wall ball and lunge stations.

Progression: Master goblet depth and RDL hinge before adding weight. Add 2 reps per set when RPE 6, then load. This session should feel doable every week.

RPE: Working sets RPE 6–7 — 2–3 reps left in the tank; form beats load.

Substitutions: Match the written effort, rest, and rep scheme. Substitutions are for missing equipment — not to make the session easier.

--- SESSION ---

Warm-up:
5 min brisk walk · Goblet squat × 10, RDL × 10 (light)

Main set:
Goblet Squat — 4×12

Block 2:
DB Romanian Deadlift — 4×10

Block 3:
DB Floor Press — 3×12

Block 4:
Dead Bug — 3×10 each side

Cool-down:
5 min stretch — hips and hamstrings
