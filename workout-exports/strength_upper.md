## STR-005 — Upper Push & Pull

Category: strength_upper | Type: strength | Variant: upper
Difficulty: intermediate | Duration: 50 min
Equipment: barbell, dumbbells, pull-up bar
Focus: horizontal push, vertical pull, shoulder stability
Tags: strength, upper_body, pressing, pulling, ski_erg, build_phase, intermediate

Description: Balanced upper push and pull for SkiErg and sled work — equal row volume to every press.

Progression: HYROX upper prep: keep bench and row sets equal. Progress bench only if rows stay within 1 rep of press performance. Face pulls every session protect ski pull shoulders.

Coaching cues:
  - Long arms at the catch; snap the handle past the knees, then finish with triceps.
  - Breathe out on the pull — avoid holding breath through the drive.
  - Legs do 60% of the work; save arms for the last 250 m of each piece.
  - Damper 5–7 for most HYROX prep; higher is not always faster.
  - Ski pull is a horizontal pull under fatigue — rows at RPE 7 are race-specific.

Substitutions: Match the written effort, rest, and rep scheme. Substitutions are for missing equipment — not to make the session easier.

No SkiErg: row the same distance/time at the same RPE. Bike: 2 min moderate ≈ 500 m ski in conditioning blocks.

--- SESSION ---

Warm-up:
Band pull-aparts × 15 · Push-up × 10 · Scapular pull-ups × 8

Main set:
Bench Press — 4×8 @ RPE 7

Block 2:
Barbell Row — 4×8

Block 3:
DB Shoulder Press — 3×10

Block 4:
Face Pulls — 3×15

Cool-down:
Chest and lat stretch

## STR-008 — Overhead & Core

Category: strength_upper | Type: strength | Variant: upper
Difficulty: intermediate | Duration: 45 min
Equipment: dumbbells, barbell_optional, wall_ball
Focus: overhead stability, core anti-rotation, shoulder endurance
Tags: strength, overhead, wall_ball, core, hyrox_station, build_phase, intermediate

Description: Overhead stability for wall balls — pair every press with scapular control for shoulder durability.

Progression: Wall ball sets: add 3 reps per week before load. Pair every push-heavy set with scapular work — add 1×12 band pull-aparts if shoulders feel tight.

RPE: Push press RPE 7–8; wall balls RPE 7 (unbroken sets); get-ups RPE 6 technique focus.

Coaching cues:
  - Full squat every rep — no shallow cuts when tired.
  - Catch the ball softly at chest height; don't let it drop to the waist.
  - Breathe: exhale on throw, quick inhale on catch.
  - Break sets before form breaks — 15 unbroken beats 30 sloppy.

Substitutions: Match the written effort, rest, and rep scheme. Substitutions are for missing equipment — not to make the session easier.

No wall ball: sandbag thrusters or goblet squat to press — same reps, controlled tempo.

--- SESSION ---

Warm-up:
PVC pass-throughs × 10 · Wall ball × 15 (light) · Dead bug × 8

Main set:
Push Press or DB Push Press — 5×5

Block 2:
Wall Ball — 5×15 (controlled unbroken sets)

Block 3:
Turkish Get-Up — 2×3 each side (light)

Block 4:
Hollow Hold — 3×30 sec

Cool-down:
Shoulder mobility
