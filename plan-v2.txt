
========== WEEK 1 (BASE) ==========

  Mon — Lower Strength + HYROX  [60 min]
  Coach: Build the lift first, then carry that strength into the workout. Focus on clean movement.
    Warm-up:
      - Easy bike or row: 3 min
      - Air squats: 2x10
      - Walking lunges: 2x10
      - Leg swings: 30s each side
    Strength:
      - Back Squat: 4x8 — Hard but smooth — leave 2 reps in reserve
      - Romanian Deadlift: 3x10-12
    Workout (For time):
      - Run: 600m
      - Sled Push: 50m
      - Walking Lunges: 20 reps
    Core (3 rounds):
      - Hanging leg raises: 10 reps
      - Plank: 45s
      - Dead bug: 30s
    Cool-down:
      - Easy walk or row: 3-5 min
      - Static stretching: 5-10 min

  Tue — Easy Aerobic Run  [50 min]
  Coach: Run/walk is the plan — alternate as prescribed and keep the running easy. You are building toward continuous running.
    Warm-up:
      - Brisk walk: 5 min
      - Leg swings: 30s each side
      - Ankle circles & easy skips: 1 min
    Run (Ease in — walking is part of the plan.):
      - Run/Walk: Run 1 min / Walk 2 min × 8
    Cool-down:
      - Easy walk or row: 3-5 min
      - Static stretching: 5-10 min

  Wed — HYROX Express  [30 min]
  Coach: Quick session — steady pace, clean technique on every station.
    Warm-up:
      - Easy jog: 5 min
      - Dynamic leg swings: 30s each
      - Strides: 3x50m
    Workout (4 rounds, steady pace):
      - Run: 400m
      - SkiErg: 250m
    Cool-down:
      - Easy walk or row: 3-5 min
      - Static stretching: 5-10 min

  Thu — Upper Strength + HYROX  [60 min]
  Coach: Build the lift first, then carry that strength into the workout. Focus on clean movement.
    Warm-up:
      - Easy ski or row: 3 min
      - Push-ups: 2x10
      - Band pull-aparts: 2x15
      - Shoulder circles: 30s each direction
    Strength:
      - Bench Press: 4x8 — Hard but smooth — leave 2 reps in reserve
      - Strict Pull-ups: 3x10-12
    Workout (For time):
      - SkiErg: 250m
      - Farmers Carry: 100m
      - Row: 250m
    Core (3 rounds):
      - Hanging leg raises: 10 reps
      - Plank: 45s
      - Dead bug: 30s
    Cool-down:
      - Easy walk or row: 3-5 min
      - Static stretching: 5-10 min

  Fri — Running Intervals  [40 min]
  Coach: Gentle speed exposure. Stay relaxed — this is about smooth turnover, not max effort.
    Warm-up:
      - Brisk walk: 5 min
      - Leg swings: 30s each side
      - Ankle circles & easy skips: 1 min
    Intervals (Gentle speed — stay relaxed and smooth, this is not all-out.):
      - Strides: 6 × 20 sec relaxed strides, walk back to recover
    Cool-down:
      - Easy walk or row: 3-5 min
      - Static stretching: 5-10 min
